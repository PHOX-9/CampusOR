# CampusOR Color Palette

This palette is designed for clarity, trust, and accessibility in campus and public-service environments.

---

## Brand Colors

### Primary
- **Campus Blue** — `#2563EB`
  - Primary actions (Join Queue, Confirm)
  - Active states
  - Key highlights

### Secondary / Accent
- **Slate Navy** — `#1E293B`
  - Headings
  - Primary text
  - Navigation elements

---

## Background & Surface

- **Surface Gray** — `#F8FAFC`
  - App background
  - Kiosk and dashboard canvases

- **White** — `#FFFFFF`
  - Cards
  - Modals
  - Foreground containers

---

## Status Colors

- **Success (Open)** — `#16A34A`
  - Queue open
  - Live counters

- **Warning (Paused)** — `#D97706`
  - Queue paused
  - Medium wait states

- **Danger (Full / Closed)** — `#DC2626`
  - Capacity full
  - Closed counters

---

## Text Colors

- **Primary Text** — `#1E293B`
- **Secondary Text** — `#475569`
- **Muted / Meta Text** — `#94A3B8`

---

## Accessibility Notes

- All text meets WCAG AA contrast standards
- Status colors are paired with labels/icons (not color-only meaning)
- Palette works in light mode and adapts cleanly to dark mode
